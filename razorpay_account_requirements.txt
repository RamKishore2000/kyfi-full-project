KYFI - Details Required From Client For Razorpay Account Creation
=================================================================

Purpose
-------
We will create the Razorpay account for KYFI subscription payments.
Please provide the below business details and documents so we can complete the
Razorpay account setup and KYC activation.


1. Basic Business Details Required
----------------------------------
Please provide:

1. Business / company / shop name.
2. Owner / authorized person full name.
3. Business mobile number.
4. Business email ID.
5. Business address.
6. City.
7. State.
8. PIN code.
9. Business type:
   - Individual
   - Proprietorship
   - Partnership
   - LLP
   - Private Limited Company
10. GST number, if available.
11. PAN number.


2. KYFI Payment Details
-----------------------
Please confirm:

1. Final yearly subscription price.
2. Business name to show on Razorpay payment page.
3. Support email to show to customers.
4. Support mobile number to show to customers.


3. Bank Details Required For Settlement
---------------------------------------
Please provide bank details where Razorpay should send payment settlements:

1. Account holder name.
2. Bank account number.
3. IFSC code.
4. Bank name.
5. Branch name.
6. Cancelled cheque or bank passbook front page or recent bank statement.


4. Documents Required Based On Business Type
--------------------------------------------
Please provide documents based on your business type.


A. If Individual / Proprietorship
---------------------------------
Please provide:

1. Owner PAN card.
2. Owner Aadhaar card.
3. GST certificate, if GST registered.
4. Shop / business registration proof, if available.
5. Bank proof:
   - Cancelled cheque, or
   - Bank statement, or
   - Passbook front page.
6. Business address proof, if available.


C. If LLP / Private Limited Company
-----------------------------------
Please provide:

1. Company PAN card.
2. Certificate of Incorporation.
3. GST certificate, if GST registered.
4. MOA / AOA or LLP agreement.
5. Board resolution / authorization letter, if available.
6. PAN card of authorized director/signatory.
7. Aadhaar card of authorized director/signatory.
8. Bank proof in company/LLP name.
9. Registered office address proof.


5. OTP / Verification Requirement
---------------------------------
During Razorpay account creation and KYC verification, OTP may come to:

1. Registered business mobile number.
2. Registered business email ID.
3. Aadhaar-linked mobile number, if Aadhaar verification is required.

Please be available to share OTP during account setup.


7. Final Checklist To Send Us
-----------------------------
Please send:

1. Business name.
2. Owner/authorized person name.
3. Business mobile number.
4. Business email ID.
5. Business address.
6. Business type.
7. PAN number and PAN card copy.
8. Aadhaar card copy of owner/authorized person.
9. GST certificate, if available.
10. Bank account details.
11. Bank proof/cancelled cheque.
12. Business registration proof, if available.
13. Final yearly subscription price.
14. Support email.
15. Support mobile number.


Important Note
--------------
Razorpay may ask for additional documents during KYC review depending on the
business type and compliance checks. If Razorpay asks for any extra document, we
will inform you immediately.
